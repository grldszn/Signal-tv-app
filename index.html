About geo-blocking

Channels like BBC iPlayer, beIN Sports, Sky Sports, Canal+, and others restrict streams by country. The proxy helps bypass CORS errors but cannot replace a VPN for true geo-unblocking. For best results with geo-blocked content, use a VPN at the OS/network level.
